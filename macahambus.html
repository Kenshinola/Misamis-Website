Macahambus Cave and Gorge

Macahambus Cave and Gorge is a natural wonder located in Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental, Philippines. The cave system offers a unique underground experience with stunning stalactites and stalagmites, while the gorge provides breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. It is a favorite spot for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts looking for a day of exploration and discovery.

🌄 Tourism & Local Importance

Macahambus Cave and Gorge plays a significant role in promoting eco-tourism in the region. It serves as a vital green space for locals and visitors alike, offering recreational activities and educational opportunities about the natural environment. The site contributes to the local economy by attracting tourists and supporting businesses in the surrounding area.
