Xavier University – Ateneo de Cagayan

A prestigious Jesuit university established in 1933, renowned for academic excellence, social commitment, and a vibrant campus life in the heart of Cagayan de Oro.

📍 Campus & Identity

Xavier University — also known as Ateneo de Cagayan — is a private Jesuit institution in Cagayan de Oro City with a rich history spanning over 90 years. It offers programs in humanities, sciences, engineering, business, and health sciences. With state-of-the-art facilities and a beautiful campus, XU is a cornerstone of higher education in Northern Mindanao and a significant contributor to the cultural and intellectual life of the region.
