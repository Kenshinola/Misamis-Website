Laguindingan Airport

Laguindingan Airport, also known as Lumbia Airport, is the main airport serving Cagayan de Oro City and the surrounding areas in Misamis Oriental, Philippines. It is located in the municipality of Laguindingan, approximately 46 kilometers from Cagayan de Oro City. The airport features modern facilities and serves as a gateway for both domestic and international travelers visiting the region. With its strategic location and efficient services, Laguindingan Airport plays a crucial role in promoting tourism and economic development in Misamis Oriental.

🌄 Tourism & Local Importance

Laguindingan Airport plays a significant role in promoting tourism and economic growth in the region. It serves as a vital transportation hub for both locals and visitors, facilitating travel to and from Cagayan de Oro City and other destinations in Misamis Oriental. The airport contributes to the local economy by attracting tourists, supporting businesses, and providing employment opportunities for the community.
