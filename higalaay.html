Higalaay Festival

The Higalaay Festival, also known as the Cagayan de Oro Fiesta, is an annual celebration held in Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental, Philippines. The festival takes place every August and is a vibrant showcase of the city's culture, traditions, and community spirit. It features a variety of events, including street dancing competitions, parades, concerts, and religious activities. The Higalaay Festival is a time for locals and visitors to come together and celebrate the rich heritage of Cagayan de Oro while enjoying the lively atmosphere and festivities that the city has to offer.

🌄 Tourism & Local Importance

The Higalaay Festival plays a significant role in promoting cultural tourism in the region. It serves as a vital educational space for locals and visitors alike, offering recreational activities and educational opportunities about the cultural heritage of the area. The festival contributes to the local economy by attracting tourists and supporting businesses in the surrounding area.
