Mapawa Nature Park

Mapawa Nature Park is a popular eco-tourism destination located in Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental, Philippines. Situated about 30 kilometers from the city center, the park offers a lush natural environment with waterfalls, caves, and hiking trails. It is a favorite spot for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and families looking for a day of outdoor fun. The park features activities such as trekking, rappelling, and swimming in natural pools formed by cascading waterfalls.

🌄 Tourism & Local Importance

Mapawa Nature Park plays a significant role in promoting eco-tourism in the region. It serves as a vital green space for locals and visitors alike, offering recreational activities and educational opportunities about the natural environment. The park contributes to the local economy by attracting tourists and supporting businesses in the surrounding area.
