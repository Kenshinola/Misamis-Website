El Salvador City

Known as the "City of Mercy," El Salvador sits along the coast of Macajalar Bay and is celebrated for its spiritual landmarks, coastal charm, and warm community spirit.

🗺️ Geography & Location

El Salvador City sits on the western coast of Misamis Oriental, bordering Macajalar Bay to the north, Opol to the east, Alubijid to the west, and Naawan to the south. Composed of 15 barangays, the city is conveniently located about 15–45 minutes by road from Cagayan de Oro, and just over 25 km from Laguindingan International Airport.

🛶 Economy & Lifestyle

The economy is primarily driven by commerce, fishing, and tourism. The city's coastal location supports a thriving fishing industry, while its proximity to Cagayan de Oro provides access to larger commercial markets. The lifestyle blends urban convenience with coastal charm, underpinned by a strong sense of community and cultural pride.

🧭 Quick Facts

El Salvador City is a 6th class component city with a steadily growing population. Its coastal location and spiritual attractions make it a unique blend of pilgrimage destination and community-oriented city in Northern Mindanao.

Attractions & Places to Visit

Divine Mercy Shrine

A serene religious site featuring a large statue of Jesus and panoramic views of the city and bay.

Our Lady of Snows Parish Church

A beautiful church known for its unique architecture and religious significance.
