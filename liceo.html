Liceo De Cagayan University

Established in 1955, Liceo de Cagayan University is a premier institution of learning in Northern Mindanao — nurturing students to excel academically and become socially responsible leaders.

📍 Campus & Identity

Founded by Atty. Rodolfo N. Pelaez and Elsa Pelaez-Pelaez, Liceo began as a professional and law school before growing into a full university. It now offers a wide array of programs including health and medical sciences, engineering and technology, business and law, education, and the arts. Its motto — Nil Sine Numine ("Nothing without Divine Will") — reflects the university's commitment to holistic development, social responsibility, and community engagement.
