Cagayan De Oro College – PHINMA

Part of the PHINMA Education network, this institution has been making quality education accessible since 1948 — providing affordable, industry-relevant courses to students across the region.

📍 Campus & Identity

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College traces its roots to 1948, when it was founded as Parent-Teacher College to provide accessible education to youth and working adults. It became part of the PHINMA Education Network in 2005 under the PHINMA Group — an organization committed to making lives better through broader access and improved academic quality. Today it offers programs in business, information technology, health sciences, and education.
