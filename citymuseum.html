City Museum

City Museum is a cultural institution located in Cagayan de Oro City, Misamis Oriental, Philippines. It houses a collection of artifacts, exhibits, and interactive displays that showcase the history, culture, and heritage of the region. The museum serves as an educational hub for locals and visitors alike, offering insights into the rich traditions and stories of Misamis Oriental. It is a must-visit destination for those interested in learning about the diverse cultural tapestry of the area and its historical significance.

🌄 Tourism & Local Importance

City Museum plays a significant role in promoting cultural tourism in the region. It serves as a vital educational space for locals and visitors alike, offering recreational activities and educational opportunities about the cultural heritage of the area. The site contributes to the local economy by attracting tourists and supporting businesses in the surrounding area.
