Tiklas Falls

Tiklas Falls is a picturesque waterfall located in the municipality of Balingasag, Misamis Oriental, Philippines. It is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers, offering a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The falls are surrounded by lush greenery and provide a refreshing swimming spot for visitors. Tiklas Falls is an ideal location for picnics, hiking, and enjoying the beauty of nature in Misamis Oriental.

🌄 Tourism & Local Importance

Tiklas Falls plays a significant role in promoting eco-tourism in the region. It serves as a vital green space for locals and visitors alike, offering recreational activities and educational opportunities about the natural environment. The site contributes to the local economy by attracting tourists and supporting businesses in the surrounding area.
